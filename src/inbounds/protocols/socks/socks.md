Standard Socks protocol inbound, compatible with [Socks 4](http://ftp.icm.edu.pl/packages/socks/socks4/SOCKS4.protocol), [Socks 4a](https://ftp.icm.edu.pl/packages/socks/socks4/SOCKS4A.protocol), Socks 5, and **HTTP**.

**DANGER**: The Socks protocol does not encrypt transmission and is not suitable for the public internet.

[Documentation ↗](https://xtls.github.io/en/config/inbounds/socks.html)
