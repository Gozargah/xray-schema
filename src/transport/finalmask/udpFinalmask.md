UDP FinalMask layers are applied in order, with the first item being the outermost camouflage layer.

Used together with `raw` UDP, `kcp`, `hysteria`, and `xhttp` H3.

Supported UDP layer types:

- `header-custom`
- `header-dns`
- `header-dtls`
- `header-srtp`
- `header-utp`
- `header-wechat`
- `header-wireguard`
- `mkcp-original`
- `mkcp-aes128gcm`
- `noise`
- `salamander`
- `sudoku`
- `xdns`
- `xicmp`
- `realm`
